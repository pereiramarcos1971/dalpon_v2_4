Snow plowing is the process of removing snow from roads, driveways, parking lots, and other outdoor areas using specialized equipment called snow plows. These vehicles are equipped with blades or shovels that push or lift the snow to clear the designated area. Snow plowing is typically performed by professional snow removal companies or municipal services during the winter months when snowfall accumulates on the ground.
